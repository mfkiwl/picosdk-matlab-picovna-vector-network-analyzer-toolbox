Troubleshooting

This section covers some troubleshooting information for common errors that may be encountered.

Contents

Unable to connect to a device

  • Check that the device is connected to the PC and listed in the Windows Device Manager.
  • Confirm that one of the two LEDs on the front of the device are illuminated.
  • Ensure that no other instance of a device object corresponding to a previous connection is present - if so, delete it.

Attempt to connect to a device results in a lockup

This can occur if a session has been previously terminated using Ctrl-C and the disconnectVNA function has not been called.

  • Disconnect the USB cable from the PicoVNA and close MATLAB
  • Reconnect the USB lead

General

NOTE: Always call the disconnectVNA function on the device object on completion of data aquisition.
